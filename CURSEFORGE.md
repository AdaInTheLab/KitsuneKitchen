# KitsuneKitchen

A cooking data pack for Hytale that adds 12 custom food items with unique buffs, crafted at the Campfire and Chef's Stove.

---

## Overview

KitsuneKitchen expands Hytale's food system with a full progression of recipes — from simple campfire snacks to powerful feast dishes that grant combat buffs. Every item has a custom icon, styled tooltip, and meaningful gameplay effects.

---

## Features

**3 Campfire Recipes (Tier 1)**
- Baked Potato — Restores 5% health
- Grilled Corn — Restores 5% health
- Fried Egg — Restores 5% health

**6 Chef's Stove Recipes (Tier 2)**
- Fries — Restores 10% health
- Tomato Soup — Restores 10% health, Hearty buff (15 min)
- Mushroom Soup — Restores 10% health, Hearty buff (15 min)
- Veggie Stir Fry — Restores 10% health, Energized buff (15 min)
- Hamburger — Restores 10% health, Hearty buff (15 min)
- Steak & Baked Potato — Restores 10% health, Hearty buff (15 min)

**3 Chef's Stove Recipes (Tier 3)**
- Kimchi — Restores 15% health, Spicy buff (30 min)
- Stuffed Aubergine — Restores 15% health, Spicy buff (30 min)
- Feast Pie — Restores 15% health, Feast buff (45 min)

**4 Unique Buffs**
- Hearty — +10% Max Health, Health Regen
- Energized — +20% Max Stamina, Stamina Regen
- Spicy — +30% Max Stamina, Stamina Regen, Physical Resistance
- Feast — +20% Max Health, +15% Max Stamina, All Regen, Damage Resistance

**12 Custom Icons** designed to match Hytale's art style.

---

## Installation

1. Download the KitsuneKitchen folder
2. Place it in your server's `game/mods/` directory
3. Restart the server

```
game/
  mods/
    KitsuneKitchen/
      manifest.json
      Common/
      Server/
```

---

## Requirements

- Hytale Early Access server
- No other mods required — fully standalone

---

## Crafting Ingredients

All recipes use base game ingredients — crops, meats, and spices found through normal gameplay. No custom resources to hunt down.
